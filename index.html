What is ADev Launcher?

ADev Launcher is the unified home for every game built under ADev Studios. It brings your library together in a clean, modern interface designed with intention, speed, and premium UX at its core. No clutter. No noise. Just your games — beautifully presented.

Why We Built It

We wanted a launcher that felt alive — something that matched the identity of our studio and the worlds we create. ADev Launcher is built to be fast, expressive, and cross‑platform, supporting both Linux and Windows with a consistent, polished experience.
