What is ADev Launcher?

ADev Launcher is the unified home for every game built under ADev Studios...but it can launch your game too! Almost any executable, can launch as well.

Why We Built It

We wanted a launcher that felt alive... and provided a seamless experience with different games. We have some themes, but we're always looking to improve! Adding games is really easy!
